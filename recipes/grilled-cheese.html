Grilled Cheese

Description

When it comes down to a snack that can satisfy you at any point in the day, you have to make sure you know how to make a grilled cheese sandwich. Can be made within minutes, and needs little effort, it is a great meal for all.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter up the bread on one side and place bread on a hot skillet, with butter side facedown.
  2. Add a slice of cheese to the top of the bread, then add another slice of bread on top.
  3. Cook the sandwich until the bottom slice is brown, then flip.
  4. Continue to cook until cheese is melted.
